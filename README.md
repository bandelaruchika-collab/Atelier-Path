🌸 Fashion Career Hub

A platform designed to help fashion students discover job opportunities and build their careers.

📌 Project Overview

Fashion Career Hub is a website created for fashion students who struggle to find job and internship opportunities in the fashion industry. The platform bridges the gap between students and the fashion job market by providing curated listings, career guidance, and mentorship support.

🎯 Objective
Help fashion students explore career opportunities
Provide structured guidance for job preparation
Connect students with mentors and alumni
Increase awareness of internships and industry roles
🎨 Design System

Color Palette:

Blush Pink
Lavender

Typography:

Editorial Serif (elegant, fashion-inspired look)

Layout Style:

Bento Grid (modular, clean, visually appealing sections)
🚀 Key Features
💼 Job & Internship Listings
Curated opportunities in fashion design, styling, merchandising, and more
Filters based on role, location, and experience
📚 Career Guides & Resources
Resume and portfolio tips
Interview preparation guides
Industry insights
🤝 Mentor / Alumni Directory
Connect with experienced professionals
Gain real-world advice and networking opportunities
📅 Events & Application Deadlines
Fashion events, workshops, and webinars
Important job and internship deadlines
📊 Job Placement Insights
Track number of job placements
Visual representation of student success rates
🧑‍💻 Target Audience
Fashion design students
Recent graduates in fashion
Aspiring professionals in the fashion industry
🛠️ Tech Stack (Example)
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Framework: React (optional)
Backend: Node.js / Firebase (optional)
Database: MongoDB / Firestore
📈 Future Scope
AI-based job recommendations
Portfolio review feature
Internship application tracker
Community discussion forum
💡 Inspiration

This project is inspired by the lack of centralized platforms specifically tailored for fashion students and aims to simplify their career journey.
